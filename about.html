About Jezna Overseas Education Consultancy

Jezna Overseas Education Consultancy, established in 2018, has been dedicated to guiding students toward fulfilling their dreams of studying abroad. With a student-centric approach, we provide personalized counseling, transparent admission processes, and expert guidance on university selection, test preparation, scholarships, and visa documentation. Our strong network of reputed international universities ensures diverse academic opportunities, while our proven track record highlights hundreds of successful admissions. Beyond securing placements, we extend support with accommodation, orientation, and post-arrival assistance, making the transition to overseas education seamless. At Jezna, we believe in turning aspirations into achievements by offering genuine advice, affordable solutions, and unwavering support throughout every step of the journey.
